Executive Summary

Regulation Overview

Title: Tax Abatement Rules of Chapter 62

Description: Rules governing the new real property tax incentive program.

Regulated Entities

  • Property Owners: Owners of rental buildings receiving tax benefits.
  • Rental Buildings: Buildings where at least 50% of units charge affordable rents.

Regulating & Enforcing Authorities

Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD): Responsible for proposing, implementing, and enforcing regulations.

Stakeholders

  • Tenants: Residents affected by the tax incentive program.
  • Contractors: Engaged in construction for eligible buildings.

Key Events & Processes

  • Regulation Enactment: Local Law 122 (2024) adopting the tax benefit program.
  • Compliance Process: Property owners must apply for a Certificate of Eligibility and Reasonable Cost.
  • Violation Event: Misuse of tax benefits or non-compliance with reporting requirements.
  • Non-Compliance Consequence: Revocation of tax benefits.

Defined Conditions

  • Income Limits: Rent must be affordable to households below 80% of Area Median Income.
  • Construction Timeline: Eligible construction must be completed within specified dates.

Commitments & Obligations

Duty to Notify Tenants: Property owners must inform tenants about upcoming construction.

Rights & Benefits

Tax Abatement: Property owners may receive tax relief for eligible construction costs.

Penalties & Sanctions

Loss of Tax Benefits: Penalties imposed for non-compliance.
